I'm Sebastian and I'm a front end developer with a terrible hunger to learn and create.

Front end and web development married two sides of my life that I love, the visuals aspect and the code. My family has always been very artistic, while I was the least artistic, I would doodle and draw every so often. Front end development is great because it provides a creative canvas and uses what I like to learn.

I graduated in software engineering at University of British Columbia, but I mostly self taught myself what I know about web development. I liked how there are so many things to try out on the web (kind of like new toys) and how the web can support such experimentation.

I also love to play video games in my spare. They're another great example of both sides, the visuals aspect and the code, interwining with one another.
